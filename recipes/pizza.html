Pizza Recipe

Description

This pizza recipe is simple yet delicious. It's made with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, basil, olives, and a touch of garlic. You can add extra cheese, mushrooms, or even pepperoni for extra flavor. Enjoy your pizza!

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Pizza Sauce

  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 small red onion (sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Add the warm water and olive oil. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
  2. Place the dough in a greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise for 30-45 minutes, or until doubled in size.
  3. Divide the dough into 8 equal-sized balls. Place them on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown on top.
  4. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Remove the baked pizza from the baking sheet and transfer them to a wire rack to cool slightly.
  5. In a large skillet, heat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped garlic and cook for 1 minute, stirring occasionally. Add the dried oregano and basil, and cook for 1 more minute, stirring.
  6. In a medium bowl, whisk together the tomato sauce, olive oil, garlic, oregano, and basil. Add the shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, and mix well. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Add the pizza sauce to the prepared pizza and toss gently to combine. Arrange the pepperoni, mushrooms, bell pepper, red onion, and fresh basil leaves on top, if desired.
  8. Bake the pizza for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown on top.
  9. Enjoy your delicious pizza! If you'd like to add more toppings, simply replace the sauce and cheese mixture with your desired toppings.
