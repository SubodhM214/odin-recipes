Lasagna Recipe

Description

A classic Italian dish made with pasta, cheese, and tomatoes, this recipe is a must-try for any family or friends.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Fontina cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Gorgonzola cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Manche

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or foil.
  2. In a large skillet, heat 1/2 cup of olive oil over medium heat. Add the lasagna noodles and cook for 10 minutes, until al dente.
  3. Add the mozzarella, Parmesan, and shredded cheeses to the skillet and cook for 1 minute, until melted.
  4. Stir in the eggs, salt, and pepper, and cook for 2 more minutes.
  5. Transfer the lasagna to the prepared baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and the noodles are golden brown.
  6. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
